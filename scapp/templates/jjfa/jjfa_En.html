Micro credit training for commercial banks

Author：admin   Published：2013-01-22 13:11:46

Background：

Micro credit is a international mature financial product, it means , it is designed for lower-middle class and small businesses, commercial and sustainable microfinance. micro-credits and promotion of the use of technology is important innovation in financial markets in recent years, the financial sector in China's large-scale use, will fundamentally solve the lower-middle class and small businesses is difficult to obtain traditional mortgage servicing difficulties, will also become small and medium financial institutions future main products.

Micro-credit products mature, experienced three major stages: non-formal financial institutions to explore; welfare micro loans exploration; system of micro-credits mature technology. System of micro-credit program to achieve sustainable business operations, in the promotion of small micro-lending institutions to focus on the financial sector at the same time, can improve the micro-finance industry specialization and service efficiency.

Training target:

Improve commercial banks' core competitiveness and sustainable development, micro-credit services to ensure carry out a comprehensive and smoothly, cultivate an independent business, effective risk control, in-depth market development, excellent finish quality performance core team.

Training objects:

The main training objects are micro-credit managers, micro-credit loan officer and background office staff.

Training methods:

Micro-credit personnel training and training system is a micro-lending business management and team building important content, micro-lending business to take full training mode, to take a combination of theory and practice training methods.

Theoretical training:

The micro credit officer together systematic theoretical training. Relates to micro-credit loan business processes, financial literacy, banking and quality training, such as: communication skills, time management and so on. Training also combines teaching, role-playing games and group discussions to enhance the students' initiative and participation. Classroom training courses, content, trainers should be relatively fixed, the training time is shorter, usually one to two weeks. After the end of the course will be tested.

Note the following rules for classroom training:

Lecturers to prepare in advance and interactive lecture topics, lecture content should be clear framework, highlighting key points, and with the practice of micro-lending business positions practicality;

Curriculum is necessary to pay attention to the issue of convergence between the content, but also to avoid or reduce the core content of the cross or duplicate;

Lecturers should create lively interactive atmosphere, loan officers continue to guide thinking and active participation;

Instructor should guide students to participate in established between theoretical training to help each other mutual encouragement and mutual exchanges of good relations, training to enhance self-esteem and team spirit of win-win cooperation.

Practice training:

Practice training mainly use Apprenticeship. Trainees follow microfinance trainers field practice. Trainee Trainer hand to explain the work content, work steps, answering their questions, targeted assessment and counseling. On the other hand observe trainee's performance at work, regular assessment.

Theme training:

Theme training for the actual work process problems, organize small seminars and discussions. Boot microfinance staff together to find answers or solutions. Special training are mostly related to the loan officer at the credit process operation problems, such as identifying the application form the intrinsic link between the various elements, such as the right to cross-examination.
